About

What is JSter?

JSter is a catalog of frontend JavaScript libraries. We also maintain [a blog](/blog/) and publish [a monthly newsletter](https://buttondown.email/jster). If you want to find a library for some specific purpose, we probably have something for you.

Origin

JSter is a spiritual successor to [JSwiki](https://github.com/bebraw/jswiki/wiki) established by [Juho Vepsäläinen](https://github.com/bebraw/). JSter started out as a collaboration between Juho and the Codegyre development team: Michael Bodnarchuk and Nick Palamarchuk. We took the technology they had developed for Symfohub and then retrofitted it. After that, Juho rewrote the site on top of a Deno based stack developed with [Gustwind](https://gustwind.js.org).
